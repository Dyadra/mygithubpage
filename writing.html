Love


Sweetheart, I’d love to trace your face
with the edge of my machete.
Honey, I want to hold your hand
and squeeze until your pulse stops .
Dear, your presence gives me comfort,
but your absence gives me joy.


DownLow


he’s a walking contradiction
forming his own
personal addiction

to the gazes, the glares
the looks
and the stares

is it mental confusion
that’s got him
performing this illusion?

or is he some striking ingénue?
back then he used to be so timid
but now he’s made his debut

as a major player in the drag scene
the way he glimmers, the way he shimmers
he’s one marvelous queen


Myraid and mixtures


Myriad and mixtures,
intangible figures
reminding me of what you said.

You’re setting up pillars,
causing all these triggers
to make me lose my head.

Expressions like scissors,
leaving me with blisters
that just won’t heal.

However, the thought still lingers
that I might be okay.
But then my faith withers
And I continue, on my way.


Her


Where was she to create a spatial illusion
Of love and togetherness, when I needed her the most?
She was making elliptical movements of coming and going
Not allowing my cries to pierce through her heart
Like an arrow of my intense yearning

Where was she to be my steady savior?
From all my wrong doings and my blunders
She’s creating her own unrelenting drive to
Procure her own greedy dreams while
Ignoring my wants and needs

Where was she to be my mother and
Protect me like a guardian angel?
She was dominating my every thought
Of rejection and alienation
Her self-indulgence is an unusual genius.
