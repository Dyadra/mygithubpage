Daily/OverDose

Everyday, a vast amount of young adults have a routine of waking up, getting ready for the day, and taking their daily does of prescribed medication. It’s become so accustomed that so many people are entirely dependent on their medication. Eventually, it gets to the point where medication has becomes as vital to one's own existence as food and water.
My goals was to make an installation that comments on the notion that medication has become a necessity for some people struggling with mental illnesses and emotional anxieties.
Model: Stephanie Ulan
Fall 2016.

La Prisma

Sound and vision have a very complex relationship with one another; they both function as modes of expression. La Prisma is an interactive installation that serves as a midi controller that interacts with projection mapping visuals. The controller is made using Arduino and Tone.JS with P5.JS serial communication. The sounds communicate with visuals that are then projection mapped.
Group Members: Shirly Huang and Guillermo Carrasquero
Fall 2016.

Police Brutality Video

VIEWER DESCRETION ADVISED. Police brutality is an issue that is embedded subversive within society. I wanted to make a statement piece commenting on the dehumanization of the victims of police brutality, especially as illustrated by the media. The video is created using Avidemux and Adobe Premier. I manipulated the original videos using a technique called datamoshing, which, involved me opening the source code of the video, and reprogramming it so that certain frames are rendered broken, giving the video can “trippy” effect. All of my source videos are found on YoutTube.
Credit to Rage Against the Machine for the cover of NWA’s F**k the Police.
Fall 2016

1933 Installation

Group Art Installation done in inspiration of a location known as 1933 Shanghai, or 上海1933老场坊 installed on the 8th floor of the NYU Shanghai Academic Building. The installation is made from cardboard, modeling clay, and plastic. The goal of the installation was to highlight the effects of the consumerism of a historic space located deep within ancient Shanghai. The space that we were inspired by had previously been built as a slaughterhouse for livestock, but has since then become a space for retail. Our project seeks to highlight the effects of capitalism on society.
Group Members: Zhan Zhang, Xiran Yang, Kevin Pham.
